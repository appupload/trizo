Installation Guide

Having trouble installing? Google Play Protect occasionally blocks apps downloaded outside the Play Store. Follow these simple steps.

Method 1: Quick Install (Recommended)

1. Tap More details
2. Tap Install without scanning

Method 2: Turn off Play Protect

1. Open Play Store & tap Avatar
2. Tap Play Protect
3. Toggle Scan apps off
4. Tap Turn off

After turning off Play Protect, you can safely install the app!
